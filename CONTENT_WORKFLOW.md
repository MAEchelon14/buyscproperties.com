# CONTENT WORKFLOW

When creating content for buyscproperties.com:

Step 1

Research the topic.

Include:

- History
- Economic development
- Population trends
- Infrastructure projects
- Business growth

Step 2

Explain why the topic matters.

Step 3

Connect it to real estate.

Residential impacts.

Commercial impacts.

Investment impacts.

Step 4

Use a feature article style.

Never write:

"Are you looking for..."

Never write:

"Contact us today!"

Never use:

"Unlock the potential"

Never use:

"Thriving market"

Never use:

"Hidden gem"

Step 5

Write as if publishing in:

- Southern Living
- Columbia Business Monthly

Step 6

Include SEO naturally.

Step 7

End with a thoughtful conclusion.

Not a sales pitch.

The goal is authority and credibility.

Not lead generation copy.
