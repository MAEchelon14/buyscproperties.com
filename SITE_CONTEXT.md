## Company Overview

Owners:

- Mary Anne Eubank

Website:
https://buyscproperties.com

Mission:

Carolina Real Estate Investment Group helps property owners, investors, and business owners buy, sell, lease, renovate, and reposition real estate throughout South Carolina.

The company combines investment experience, redevelopment expertise, and local market knowledge to identify opportunities often overlooked by traditional brokerage firms.
